Conference

The Innovation and Skill Development Research Center (ISDRC) conduct one National Conference each academic year to promote research, innovation, and skill development. It focuses on scientific and technological advancements, covering domains such as physics, engineering, materials science, electronics, data science, energy systems, nanotechnology, robotics, quantum technology and applied research. The conference brings together experts from universities, research institutes, and industry to share their work through keynote lectures, invited talks, scientific model presentation, paper presentations, and poster sessions. Through this annual conference, ISDRC aims to strengthen the research culture within the university and beyond, facilitate networking between institutions, and contribute meaningfully to India’s scientific and technological development.
