One day national seminar on

SCIENTIFIC INNOVATION AND SKILL DEVELOPMENT FOR SOCIETAL ADVANCEMENT

SISDSA-2.0 | 2026

Date: 18 March 2026 · Time: 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Venue: Dr. K. P. Basu Memorial Hall, Engineering Science Building, Jadavpur University, Kolkata - 700032

About the Conference

SISDSA 2.0 provides a collaborative platform for knowledge exchange, interdisciplinary dialogue, and capacity building. It brings together scientists, students, and industry professionals to discuss how scientific innovation and skill development in physics and allied disciplines can contribute to societal advancement and sustainable development.
