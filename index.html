Innovation & Skill Development Research Centre (ISDRC)

Department of Physics, Jadavpur University, Kolkata - 700032

The Innovation and Skill Development Research Centre (ISDRC) is an academic and technical activity centre established in 2024. It was created to promote skill-oriented education among young learners in alignment with the National Education Policy (NEP). The centre operates at the intersection of science, technology, and society, motivating students to think both creatively and logically. ISDRC actively encourages hands-on learning, experimentation, and project-based education in interdisciplinary topics. One of its key sections is Teaching and Learning, which guides students toward research opportunities and academic growth.

About ISDRC

The Innovation and Skill Development Research Centre (ISDRC) was established in the Department of Physics at Jadavpur University in 2024 to strengthen innovation and skill education as envisioned by the National Education Policy (NEP). It provides students a powerful space to showcase their work, ideas, and research. One of the unique and pioneering initiatives of ISDRC is its annual research journal, dedicated primarily to publishing full-length research papers authored by undergraduate and postgraduate students of West Bengal, while also welcoming high-quality contributions from PhD scholars and independent researchers‚Äîmaking it a completely new and first-of-its-kind concept in the region. ISDRC also houses a workshop with fabrication units equipped with instrument and prototyping tools, where students can transform theoretical ideas into functional prototypes and real-world products. From concept development to product fabrication, the centre supports the entire innovation pipeline. Beyond academics and research, ISDRC also serves as a launchpad for entrepreneurship. The centre provides a dedicated platform to support new startups, innovative business ideas, and student-led ventures, guiding them toward growth, sustainability, and real market impact.

    Vision

  • To become a pioneering hub of interdisciplinary innovation ‚Äî enabling students and young researchers to explore, experiment, and create impactful solutions across science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.
  • To cultivate a research-driven academic ecosystem ‚Äî where undergraduate and postgraduate learners gain hands-on exposure, pursue original investigations, and contribute meaningfully to scientific advancement.
  • To empower the next generation of scientists, educators, and technologists ‚Äî by providing an accessible platform for skill development, collaborative learning, and professional growth.
  • To promote scientific thinking and an innovation culture within educational institutions ‚Äî transforming students from passive learners into active innovators capable of addressing real-world challenges.
  • To emerge as a recognized academic catalyst in Eastern India ‚Äî known for fostering quality research, publishing student-driven scholarly work, and supporting entrepreneurial initiatives rooted in science and technology.

    • Mission

  • Provide structured research opportunities: Facilitate guided projects, laboratory work, simulations, and interdisciplinary experimentation for students at UG and PG levels.
  • Publish high-quality academic resources: Produce an annual research journal, abstract books, technical articles, and digital content that showcase student-led research and technical writing.
  • Offer certification-based skill programs: Deliver short courses and certifications in programming, electronics, computational physics, data science, and emerging technologies to enhance employability and research readiness.
  • Organize seminars, workshops, and training: Connect learners with domain experts from academia and industry to promote knowledge exchange and collaboration.
  • Promote entrepreneurship and employment: Guide students in idea development, business planning, prototype creation, and early-stage startup support.
  • Create an inclusive networking platform: Enable inter-institutional collaboration and peer-to-peer mentoring through events, hackathons, and research symposia.
